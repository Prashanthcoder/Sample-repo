Porsche, founded by Ferdinand Porsche in 1931 in Stuttgart, Germany, is a premier manufacturer of high-performance luxury sports cars, SUVs, and sedans. Originally an engineering office, the firm famously developed the Volkswagen Beetle before launching its first sports car, the 356, in 1948. The brand is synonymous with the iconic 911, introduced in 1963, which established the rear-engine layout. Renowned for engineering excellence, Porsche has secured over 24,000 racing victories. Today, the lineup includes the Cayenne, Macan, Panamera, and electric Taycan. Owned by Volkswagen AG, Porsche continues to blend tradition with modern innovation.
